What is OISC?

A Global Stage for
Student Research Project and Virtual Exchange

Each year, students from diverse cultural and linguistic backgrounds come together to engage in discussions on global issues of their choice, conducted entirely in English.

Participants collaborate as a team, conduct research on global challenges, lead discussions, and build meaningful international connections through these activities.

④ About Us

Who We Are

OISC is a multi-institutional, multi-country online COIL (Collaborative Online International Learning) program, founded in 2020.

Unlike typical 1-to-1 class-pair COIL, OISC brings together students from 11+ institutions across 6 countries for collaborative research, presentations, and cross-cultural exchange.

We are currently building the OISC Global Collaborative Learning Initiative as a formal academic association.
